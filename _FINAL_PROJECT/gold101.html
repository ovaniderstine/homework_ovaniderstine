Who Am I

I definitely hope this will be a cool website at some point, where I can share my favorite foods and also have it be interactive, so people don't just look at stuff but can maybe learn something cool.

The 101

Legendary food critic Jonathan Gold publishes a list of his 101 favorite restaurants every year. It's basically my food bible and I read it all the time to know where I should eat. Someimes he's right, other times he's way off, so I wanted to make my own list, in my own order.

Food Mood

Most importantly, I wanted to amke a website where people could interact with my content in some way. I defintely want to have some kind of checklist, where people can check off where they've been, but also search for where they should eat, based on what what mood they're in, AKA their "food mood".
